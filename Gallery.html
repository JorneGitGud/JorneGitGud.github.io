9-2020:

Study into light

To make art really come alive you need lighting and shading. The walls in this piece might be made out of simple grey clay, that monotony is broken when light coming through the windows hits and a range of colors appear.


This peace is greatly inspired by 19th-century painter, Francois Marius Granet.

11-2020:

Low frame rate

I love doing low frame-rate animation, when implemented correctly it can add another dimension to a piece. Leaving some space for your brain to fill in the gaps between frames, resulting in a personal experiance.


This animation is the main menu for my latest project. XAleste.
