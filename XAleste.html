XAleste

An unforgiving, classic Shmup.


Inspiration came from memories of childhood shooters, which were difficult, unforgiving, fast pased shooters.


XAleste does not aspire to be a clone of games passed, it's all about recreating the feel of '90s shooters.


Project

How and why?


Creating XAleste furthers my knowledge of software design and helps hone my skills. Learning objectives are: Preformance, scalability, easy to add content and composite reuse principle.
