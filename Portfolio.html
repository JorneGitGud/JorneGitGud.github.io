Galaxy

Quest


This uni-project was made using JavaFX with custom rendering.

Galaxy Quest is a fun and difficult game where the objective is to get a High score.

The grid is made up of nodes that only know their neighbours and what is on them.

There are comets to watch out for as well as pathfinding pirates that are out to destroy the player ship.

Once you visit all planets, a wormhole appears that will lead you to safety.

Programming language:

Java.

Frameworks & Libraries:

JavaFX 14, Java jdk 11, jUnit 5.

Tools:

IntelliJ, Trello, LucidChart, Maven.

Techniques:

UML, Agile.
